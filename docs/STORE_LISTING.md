## Full Description (max 4000 chars)
Transform your daily walks into epic adventures with Stridr!

Stridr tracks your everyday steps and maps them onto India's most spectacular trekking trails and famous marathon routes. Whether you're walking to work, around the neighborhood, or on the treadmill, every step moves you forward on a virtual journey through breathtaking Himalayan landscapes and world-famous marathon courses.

**🏔️ 24 Virtual Trails:**

*Incredible India Treks:*
Trek the mysterious Roopkund with its Skeleton Lake, brave the frozen Chadar River in Ladakh, explore the Valley of Flowers, summit Kedarkantha through snow, and 11 more authentic Indian trails.

*Classic Marathons:*
Start with a 5K, progress to 10K, conquer the Half Marathon, and ultimately complete the legendary Full Marathon (42.2km). For the ambitious: 50K and 100K Ultra challenges await!

*World City Marathons:*
Run through Tokyo's neon streets, conquer Boston's Heartbreak Hill, and finish at Berlin's Brandenburg Gate.

**✨ Features:**

•  **Monthly Challenges** - Earn badges each month! Reset and start fresh every 30 days like Apple Fitness rings
•  **15 Monthly Badges** - 7 step badges + 8 distance badges to unlock
•  **Monthly Master** - Earn 10/15 badges to become that month's master
•  **Yearly Champion** - Collect all 12 monthly masters for the ultimate badge
•  **Trail Badges** - 7 lifetime achievement badges for completing trails
•  **Real-time Step Tracking** - Seamless pedometer integration
•  **Unlock Landmarks** - Discover famous locations as you progress
•  **Daily Goals** - Set custom step goals and track streaks
•  **Beautiful Dashboard** - Weekly charts and personal records
•  **Cloud Sync** - Your progress saved securely across devices

**Start your journey today. One step at a time.**
