## Full Description (max 4000 chars)
Transform your daily walks into global adventures with Stridr! 

Stridr tracks your everyday steps and maps them onto the world's most famous hiking trails. Whether you're walking to work, the grocery store, or around the block, every step moves you forward on a virtual journey through breathtaking landscapes like the Inca Trail, Mount Everest Base Camp, and the Great Ocean Walk.

**Features:**
*   **Virtual Trails**: Choose from a curated selection of iconic trails from around the globe.
*   **Real-time Step Tracking**: Seamlessly integrates with your device's pedometer to track every step you take.
*   **Unlock Landmarks**: Reach distance milestones to unlock detailed information and photos of famous landmarks along your route.
*   **Visualize Progress**: See your journey on an interactive progress bar and map.
*   **Earn Badges**: Collect unique badges for hitting daily goals, maintaining streaks, and completing trails.
*   **Daily Goals**: Set custom daily step goals to stay motivated and healthy.
*   **Detailed Stats**: Analyze your activity with daily and weekly charts.
*   **Privacy First**: Your data stays on your device. No account required to start walking.

Stridr is unrelated to any other fitness app and focuses purely on the joy of walking and discovery. 

**Start your journey today. One step at a time.**
