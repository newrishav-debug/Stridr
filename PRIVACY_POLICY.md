# Privacy Policy for Stridr

**Last Updated: January 12, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Stridr ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Stridr, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you create an account, we may collect:
- **Name**: To personalize your experience
- **Email Address**: For account identification and authentication
- **Profile Photo**: Optional, to customize your profile
- **Password**: Stored securely on your device (for email-based accounts)

### Health and Fitness Data

With your permission, we access:
- **Step Count**: Daily step data from your device's pedometer sensor
- **Walking Activity**: Historical step data to track your trail progress

### Usage Data

We automatically collect:
- **Trail Progress**: Your current trail selection and distance covered
- **Achievements**: Badges earned and milestones reached
- **Daily Logs**: Step counts and distances for each day
- **Streak Information**: Consecutive days of activity

### User Preferences

We store your app settings including:
- Distance unit preference (kilometers or miles)
- Daily step goal
- Notification preferences
- Stride length setting

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Core App Functionality**
   - Track your steps and simulate walking famous trails
   - Calculate distance covered based on step count
   - Award badges and achievements for reaching milestones

2. **Personalize Your Experience**
   - Display your name and profile photo
   - Save your preferred settings and goals
   - Track your favorite trails

3. **Send Notifications** (with your permission)
   - Daily reminders to walk
   - Goal achievement celebrations
   - Badge unlock notifications
   - Milestone progress updates
   - Inactivity nudges to encourage walking

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only

**Important**: All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not:
- Upload your data to external servers
- Share your data with third parties
- Collect analytics or tracking data
- Use your data for advertising purposes

Your information is stored using secure local storage (AsyncStorage) on your device.

### Third-Party Authentication

If you choose to sign in with Google or Facebook:
- We only receive basic profile information (name, email, profile photo)
- We do not access your contacts, posts, or other social data
- Authentication is handled directly by Google or Facebook's secure services

---

## Health Data Privacy

We treat your health and fitness data with extra care:

- **Step data** is accessed only with your explicit permission
- This data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally
- You can delete your step history at any time by resetting your progress
- We use this data solely to provide trail tracking functionality

---

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control

You can:
- View all your stored data within the app
- Edit your profile information at any time
- Change your preferences and notification settings
- Delete your account and all associated data

### Notifications

You have full control over notifications:
- Enable or disable all notifications
- Choose specific notification types (reminders, achievements, etc.)
- Select preferred reminder times

### Data Deletion

To delete your data:
1. Go to Profile > Settings
2. Use the "Reset Progress" option to clear your trail data
3. Log out to remove session data
